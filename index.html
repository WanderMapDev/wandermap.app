🗺️ WanderMap

Privacy-first adventure mapping for Android

🔒 Your Privacy Matters

WanderMap shows where you've explored without storing your GPS coordinates or timestamps. Only road segment identifiers and regions are saved locally on your device.

🚀 Perfect For

  • Hikers tracking trail coverage
  • Cyclists mapping route exploration
  • Road trip enthusiasts
  • Anyone who values privacy
