WanderMap

Map Your Wanderings.

WanderMap colors your map as you walk, bike, or drive. Privacy-first by design: we save road segment IDs, never your GPS coordinates or timestamps.

What You Can Do

  • Watch your coverage grow in real time
  • Track progress across cities, counties, states, and countries
  • See which roads the WanderMap community has explored worldwide
  • Pick from five map themes, including a fog-of-war view
  • Export your data anytime, or delete everything with one tap

Privacy

WanderMap stores road segment identifiers locally on your device — not GPS traces. There's no account, no login, and nothing personally identifying. Coverage sharing for the community map is opt-in, anonymous, and revocable. Read the full Privacy Policy.
