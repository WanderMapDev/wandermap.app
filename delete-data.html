Delete Your Data

WanderMap stores your exploration data locally on your device. You can delete it anytime.

Delete Data on Your Device

  1. Open WanderMap
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap "Clear All Data"
  4. Confirm deletion

This deletes all visited road segments and cached data immediately. There's no way to recover it.

Crash Reports

If you've submitted any crash reports, they're automatically deleted after 90 days.

Want them deleted sooner? Email developer@wandermap.app with the subject "Delete My Crash Report Data" and we'll remove them within 7 days.
