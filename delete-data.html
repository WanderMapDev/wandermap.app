🗑️ Delete Your WanderMap Data

✅ Most Data is Already Under Your Control

WanderMap stores your exploration data locally on your device. You have complete control over this data and can delete it anytime through the app.

📱 Delete Data on Your Device

Step 1: Open WanderMap app
Step 2: Go to Settings
Step 3: Tap "Clear All Data"
Step 4: Confirm deletion

This immediately deletes:

  • All visited road segments
  • App preferences and settings
  • Cached map data
  • Any exported data files you created

📧 Crash Reports (If Any)

🕒 Automatic Deletion

All crash reports are automatically deleted after 90 days. No action needed from you.

⚡ Immediate Deletion (Optional)

Want your crash reports deleted sooner? Email us:

Email: developer@wandermap.app

Subject: "Delete My Crash Report Data"

Include: Approximate dates when you submitted reports (if known)

⏱️ Timeline

  • Local data: Deleted immediately when you use in-app deletion
  • Crash reports: Auto-deleted after 90 days, or within 7 days if you email us
  • No other data: We don't store location history, accounts, or personal data

🔄 What Happens After Deletion

  • Your exploration history will be completely erased
  • App will function like a fresh install
  • No way to recover deleted data
  • You can export your data before deletion if you want a backup

🛡️ Privacy-First Approach

WanderMap is designed to minimize data retention:

  • Most data never leaves your device
  • Crash reports are optional and auto-deleted
  • No user accounts or tracking
  • You control your own exploration data

❓ Questions?

Need help or have questions about data deletion? Email: developer@wandermap.app
